# Phase 1 Report — Market Research
**Project:** aidirectory  
**Date:** 2026-05-29  
**Status:** COMPLETE

---

## Summary

aidirectory is an AI tools discovery platform targeting the global, non-English-speaking internet audience — a segment completely ignored by the top AI directories. The platform combines a large, curated database of 47,000+ AI tools with auto country-language detection, a modern card-based UI, and dual monetization via Google AdSense and affiliate links.

The AI tools directory market is proven (TAAFT: 4.5M visits/mo, Toolify: 2.95M visits/mo) with clear, replicable monetization. Our differentiation is the multilingual-first approach: no top competitor serves non-English users, yet 60%+ of internet users are non-English speakers.

---

## Key Opportunity

**Blue ocean: multilingual AI directory.** No major competitor serves non-English users. Arabic, Spanish, Portuguese, French, Hindi, and Chinese collectively represent 3B+ internet users who currently have no native-language AI tools discovery platform.

Secondary opportunity: **affiliate-optimized UX**. Current directories leave significant affiliate revenue on the table with weak CTAs and no conversion optimization.

---

## Risks Identified

1. SEO moat of TAAFT / Toolify — mitigated by targeting non-English keyword markets
2. Listings going stale — mitigated by automated update pipeline
3. AdSense policy changes — mitigated by affiliate diversification
4. Translation quality — mitigated by human curation for top 10 languages
